# Vanta

**Vanta** is a large, unclaimed desert region, occupying the western end of the continent of [[Ewari|Ewari]]. There are few locations of interest in Vanta: Ventus, a large mercantile port city on the northwest coast, and the Venturi Oasis, an unincorporated, ungoverned settlement that acts as the only way stop on the arduous caravan route that passes between Ventus and Toznos, the capital of the Toznian Protectorate, a prosperous nation that is neighbors with the region of Venta.

### Ventus

Ventus is a sprawling port city that acts as home for a multitude of merchant houses doing business with trading partner the world over.
