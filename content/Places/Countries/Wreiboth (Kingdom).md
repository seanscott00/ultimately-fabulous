## History

#### The Calamity and the formation of the Crystal Wastes

Approximately one year ago, in 378 by the Wreiborn calendar, Wreiboth suffered tragedy as an unknown calamity occurred in and around the island, with earthquakes and chaotic magic storms rending the land, leaving large portions of the country under water, and other large portions being overgrown with large, jagged, crystalline formations, leaving the land mostly unusable. The capital city of [[|]] is gone, caught at the center of the incident, leaving the government and society in tatters. With the destruction of the palace and the loss of the majority of the royal family, the King's third son, Eidric, who had previously been 5th in the line of succession after his three older sisters and older brother, assumed the crown and the throne in exile.

Most of the population that did survive the disaster were residents of the port city of () and fishing villages along the coast. There was a monumental effort undertaken by the city guard of () to commandeer all sailing vessels in the harbor, and using them to evacuate as many people as possible as as the land shook and the magical wrath bore down on the city.

#### A People without a Country

The Wreiborn people settled in the shadow of the mercantile city-state of Ventus. A jewel of civilization on the coast of a massive desert, Ventus trades with everyone from everywhere. This focus on commerce in its purest form meant that there wasn't an enormous amount of labor needed to capitalize on the influx of refugees, looking for work. The Merchant Lords of Ventus gave leave for the Wreiborn to create refugee camps outside the city until they were able to find more permanent accommodations.

## SocioEconomics

Wreiboth is ruled by a hereditary monarchy. The current King is Eidric, son of the former King, and is currently living in exile, with the rest of his people, in and around Venta.
