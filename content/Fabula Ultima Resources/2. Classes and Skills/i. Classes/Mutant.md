## THERIOFORMS

Therioforms are mutations that grant powerful abilities; the way they manifest on
your character is completely up to you - pterotheria, for instance, might turn you
into a person-sized bat or simply give you feathered wings.

> - Once you use the Theriomorphosis Skill, the effects of your therioform(s) last until
the end of the scene; these effects also end if you die or fall unconscious, if you
perform the Guard action, or if you use the Theriomorphosis Skill again (this includes
using it through Genoclepsis).
> - You may end the effects of your Theriomorphosis at any time, but during a conflict
you can only do so on your turn, before or after an action.
> - When the scene ends, if you are under the effects of your Theriomorphosis, you may
spend 10 Mind Points to extend your current therioform(s) to the next scene you will
be a part of.

- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Amphibia.md|Amphibia]]**==<br>*(Frogs, salamanders, regenerating creatures, aquatic monsters.)*<br>You may breathe underwater and your eyes can see perfectly in dim light. When you recover Hit Points, you recover 5 additional Hit Points and you may immediately recover from a single status effect of your choice.
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Arpaktida.md|Arpaktida]]**==<br>*(Wolves, birds of prey, creatures with sharp hunting senses.)*<br>You treat your **Insight** as if it were one die size higher (to a maximum of **d12**). When you deal damage to one or more creatures for the first time during your turn, if at least one of them is in [[Crisis|Crisis]], you deal 5 extra damage to each of those creatures.
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Dynamotheria.md|Dynamotheria]]**==<br>*(Bears, dinosaurs, creatures of massive size and strength.)*<br>You treat your **Might** as if it were one die size higher (to a maximum of **d12**). When you succeed on a Check to advance or turn back a Clock, if that Check includes **Might**, you may fill or erase 1 additional section of that Clock.
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Electrophora.md|Electrophora]]**==<br>*(Electric creatures, lightning elementals, robots.)*<br>When you deal damage with an attack or spell, you may have all that damage become **bolt**. While your **unarmed strikes** deal **bolt** damage, any enemy who **loses 20 or more Hit Points** due to one of your **unarmed strikes** also suffers [[slow|slow]].
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Neurophagoida.md|Neurophagoida]]**==<br>*(Parasites, undead, creatures who feed on souls.)*<br>When you use the **Biophagy** Skill, you also recover an amount of Mind Points equal to **4 + (your Skill Level in Theriomorphosis)**.
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Placophora.md|Placophora]]**==<br>*(Crustaceans, turtles, armored creatures and vehicles.)*<br>You may treat your Defense score as being equal to **13 + (half your Skill Level in Theriomorphosis)** (you may still use your normal scores if better).
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Pneumophora.md|Pneumophora]]**==<br>*(Jet-propulsion mollusks, great winged beasts, rockets.)*<br>As the **Electophora therioform**, but the damage is **air** and the status effect is [[weak|weak]].
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Polypoda.md|Polypoda]]**==<br>*(Squids, octopi, creatures with tendrils or vines.)*<br>Your attacks with **unarmed strikes** gain **multi (3)**.
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Pterotheria.md|Pterotheria]]**==<br>*(Bats, birds, winged insects, flying creatures.)*<br>You are able to fly. In addition to the obvious advantages in mobility, your **melee** attacks can target **flying** creatures and you cannot be targeted by **melee** attacks unless the attacker is flying or is somehow able to reach flying targets. These benefits do not apply while you are unable to fly freely (such as when inside a vehicle designed for human-sized creatures, or inside a narrow corridor). Additionally, when you suffer **air**, **bolt** or **ice** damage during a conflict scene, you lose these benefits until the start of your next turn.
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Pyrophora.md|Pyrophora]]**==<br>*(Bombardier beetles, pistol shrimps, fire elementals.)*<br>As the **Electophora therioform**, but the damage is **fire** and the status effect is [[dazed|dazed]].
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Tachytheria.md|Tachytheria]]**==<br>*(Cats, gazelles, hares, creatures that are agile and quick.)*<br>You treat your **Dexterity** as if it were one die size higher (to a maximum of **d12**). When you succeed on a Check to advance or turn back a Clock, if that Check includes **Dexterity**, you may fill or erase 1 additional section of that Clock.
- ==**[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Therioforms/Toxicophora.md|Toxicophora]]**==<br>*(Spiders, snakes, toxic slimes and other venomous creatures.)*<br>As the **Electophora therioform**, but the damage is **poison** and the status effect is [[poisoned|poisoned]].
