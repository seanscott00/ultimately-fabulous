# Psychic Gifts

## The Brainwave Clock

When you first acquire the Psychic Gifts Skill, you receive a Brainwave Clock.
- Your Brainwave Clock has 4 sections, and it begins each scene empty.
- After you resolve one of your gifts, fill 1 section of your Brainwave Clock (you do so
after the effects of the Gift have been calculated and applied).
- Your Brainwave Clock determines your gifts' cost in Mind Points, as well as their
effectiveness (see above). You can still use your gifts when the Clock is full.
- Sections of your Brainwave Clock may not be filled in any way other than described
above, and they may only be erased through a change of scene or through the
Brainwave Discharge Heroic Skill (page XXX).

## Gifts

> [!list|rules no-title]
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Atmokinesis.md|Atmokinesis]]** ~ When you **deal damage**...<br>That damage becomes **air** or **bolt**, and its source deals extra damage equal to **2 + the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock**.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Clairvoyance.md|Clairvoyance]]** ~ When an NPC becomes your **focus** or when you create a **Bond** towards an NPC...<br>You ask the Game Master a single question about that NPC, and the Game Master answers truthfully; then, you describe what sensation revealed this information to you. You may only use this gift **once** on the same NPC.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Gravitokinesis.md|Gravitokinesis]]** ~ When you **deal damage**...<br>That damage becomes **earth** or **physical**, and its source deals extra damage equal to **2 + the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock**. If the source deals damage to one or more **flying** creatures, they are forced to land immediately.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Life Transference.md|Life Transference]]** ~ When you cause **one or more creatures to lose HP**...<br>Choose yourself **or** an ally who is your **focus**: if the chosen creature is in [[Crisis|Crisis]], they recover an amount of Hit Points equal to **5 + (the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock, multiplied by 5)**.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Photokinesis.md|Photokinesis]]** ~ When you **deal damage**...<br>That damage becomes **dark** or **light**, and its source deals extra damage equal to **2 + the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock**.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Psychic Backlash.md|Psychic Backlash]]** ~ After an enemy **succeeds on an Opposed Check** against you or **causes you to lose Hit Points**...<br>That enemy loses an amount of Mind Points equal to **5 + (the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock, multiplied by 5)**. Then, choose one option: that enemy suffers [[dazed|dazed]]; **or** that enemy suffers [[shaken|shaken]].
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Psychic Shield.md|Psychic Shield]]** ~ After an enemy you can see **performs an Accuracy Check or a Magic Check**...<br>You may treat your Defense and Magic Defense scores as being equal to **your current Willpower die size + (the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock, multiplied by 2)** against that Check (you may still use your normal scores if better).
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Reassuring Presence.md|Reassuring Presence]]** ~ After you **cover** an ally with the **Guard** action...<br>That ally recovers an amount of Mind Points equal to **10 + (the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock, multiplied by 5)**; if they are your focus, they also recover from a single status effect of their choice among [[dazed|dazed]], [[enraged|enraged]], and [[shaken|shaken]].
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Psychic Gifts/Thermokinesis.md|Thermokinesis]]** ~ When you **deal damage**...<br>That damage becomes **fire** or **ice**, and its source deals extra damage equal to **2 + the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock**.
