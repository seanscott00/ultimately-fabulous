# Psychic Gifts

## The Brainwave Clock

When you first acquire the Psychic Gifts Skill, you receive a Brainwave Clock.
- Your Brainwave Clock has 4 sections, and it begins each scene empty.
- After you resolve one of your gifts, fill 1 section of your Brainwave Clock (you do so
after the effects of the Gift have been calculated and applied).
- Your Brainwave Clock determines your gifts' cost in Mind Points, as well as their
effectiveness (see above). You can still use your gifts when the Clock is full.
- Sections of your Brainwave Clock may not be filled in any way other than described
above, and they may only be erased through a change of scene or through the
Brainwave Discharge Heroic Skill (page XXX).
