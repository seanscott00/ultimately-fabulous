## Symbols

> [!list|skills no-title]
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Binding.md|Symbol of Binding]]** ~ The **current** Attribute die sizes of the creature bearing this symbol can **never** be **higher** than that creature's corresponding **base** Attribute die sizes (this does **not** end any spells or effects, but it will negate their influence over the creature's Attributes).
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Creation.md|Symbol of Creation]]** ~ When the creature bearing this symbol needs to spend Inventory Points during a conflict, they may instead destroy this symbol to spend no Inventory Points.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Despair.md|Symbol of Despair]]** ~ When the creature bearing this symbol recovers Hit Points and/or Mind Points during conflicts, instead they only recover **half** the normal amount of Hit Points and/or Mind Points, respectively.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Destiny.md|Symbol of Destiny]]** ~ After the creature bearing this symbol makes a Check, if you are able to see them, you may spend 1 Fabula Point to force that creature to reroll both dice. You may only do so once per Check, and only if the Result was not a **critical success** or **critical failure**.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Elements.md|Symbol of Elements]]** ~ When you create this symbol, choose a damage type: **air**, **bolt**, **dark**, **earth**, **fire**, **ice**, **light**, or **poison**. When the creature bearing this symbol deals damage to one or more creatures, if you can see the creature bearing this symbol, you may spend an amount of Mind Points equal to **one third of the total damage being inflicted to those creatures** (including any sources of extra damage). If you do, all damage dealt this way changes type to the type chosen when you created this symbol.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Enmity.md|Symbol of Enmity]]** ~ As long as the creature bearing this symbol is in **[[Crisis|Crisis]]**, any enemies who are able to see them **must** include them among the targets of their attacks and offensive spells (<span class='fu-off'></span>), if possible.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Flux.md|Symbol of Flux]]** ~ When you create this symbol, choose a status effect: [[dazed|dazed]], [[shaken|shaken]], [[slow|slow]], or [[weak|weak]]. Then, choose one option: the creature bearing this symbol gains immunity to the chosen status effect; **or** the creature bearing this symbol loses immunity to the chosen status effect and cannot gain it.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Growth.md|Symbol of Growth]]** ~ Spells with a **target** of "Up to three creatures" cast by the creature bearing this symbol have a **target** of "Up to four creatures" instead (the creature still needs to spend additional Mind Points for a fourth target).
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Metamorphosis.md|Symbol of Metamorphosis]]** ~ When you create this symbol, choose a Species: **beast**, **construct**, **demon**, **elemental**, **monster**, **plant**, or **undead**. The creature bearing this symbol is treated as belonging to the chosen Species for the sake of Skills and effects, instead of their normal Species (this does **not** otherwise alter any of the creature's profile, such as their Affinities or any special rules they may have). This symbol **cannot** be applied to Player Characters.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Prosperity.md|Symbol of Prosperity]]** ~ When the creature bearing this symbol spends 1 Fabula Point to **invoke a Trait or Bond**, they also receive 100 zenit.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Protection.md|Symbol of Protection]]** ~ When you create this symbol, choose a damage type: **air**, **bolt**, **dark**, **earth**, **fire**, **ice**, **light**, or **poison**. The creature bearing this symbol gains Resistance to the chosen damage type.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Rebellion.md|Symbol of Rebellion]]** ~ If a **Villain** is present on the scene, any Opposed Checks made by the creature bearing this symbol trigger a **critical success** when both dice show the same number (as long as the Check is not a **fumble**).
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Rebirth.md|Symbol of Rebirth]]** ~ If the creature bearing this symbol is about to be reduced to 0 Hit Points, they may destroy this symbol: if they do, they are instead reduced to exactly 1 Hit Point.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Revenge.md|Symbol of Revenge]]** ~ When a creature in [[Crisis|Crisis]] hits the creature bearing this symbol with an attack or offensive spell (<span class='fu-off'></span>), the creature in [[Crisis|Crisis]] recovers 5 Hit Points and 5 Mind Points.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Sacrifice.md|Symbol of Sacrifice]]** ~ When the creature bearing this symbol suffers damage, if you are able to see them, you may destroy this symbol: if you do, you suffer the same amount of damage in place of that creature.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Sorcery.md|Symbol of Sorcery]]** ~ The total Mind Point cost of spells that target the creature bearing this symbol is reduced by 5 MP (to a minimum total cost of 5 MP). If a spell targets multiple creatures bearing this symbol, the effect will be **cumulative**.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Truth.md|Symbol of Truth]]** ~ Checks performed to examine the creature bearing this symbol, as well as Accuracy Checks and Magic Checks performed against the creature bearing this symbol, gain a +2 bonus.
> - **[[Fabula Ultima Resources/2. Classes and Skills/iv. Subskills/Symbolism/Symbol of Weakness.md|Symbol of Weakness]]** ~ When you create this symbol, choose a damage type: **air**, **bolt**, **dark**, **earth**, **fire**, **ice**, **light**, or **poison**. The creature bearing this symbol suffers 5 extra damage from sources dealing damage of the chosen type.
