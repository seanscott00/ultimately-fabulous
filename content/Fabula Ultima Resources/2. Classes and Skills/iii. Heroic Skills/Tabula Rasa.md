> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[Commander|Commander]] Class.

After you use [[Bishop's Edict|Bishop's Edict]] or [[King's Castle|King's Castle]], if you are in [[Crisis|Crisis]], you may have every
enemy creature present on the scene lose 10 Hit Points. This amount increases to 20
Hit Points if you are **level 20 or higher**, or 30 Hit Points if you are **level 40 or higher**.
