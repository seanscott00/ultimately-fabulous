> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[Weaponmaster]] Class.

After you hit a creature with [[Breach]], if you attacked with a **melee** weapon that belongs
to the **[[heavy]]** Category, you may have the attack deal damage equal to your **High Roll
(HR)** instead of dealing no damage (any source of extra damage, including the extra
damage from the third option of the **Breach** Skill itself, still applies).
