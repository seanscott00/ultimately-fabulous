> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]] Class.

While you have one or more **melee [[dagger|dagger]]** weapons equipped, you gain a +1 bonus
to Defense and Magic Defense, and you treat them as also belonging to the **[[brawling|brawling]]**,
**[[sword|sword]]**, and **[[thrown|thrown]]** Categories for the purpose of Skills and effects.

Additionally, your attacks with weapons that belong to the **dagger** Category may target
creatures that cannot normally be targeted by **melee** attacks.
