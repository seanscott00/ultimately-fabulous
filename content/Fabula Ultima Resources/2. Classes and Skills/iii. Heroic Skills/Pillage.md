> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Rogue|Rogue]] Class, and must have acquired the
[[Soul Steal|Soul Steal]] Skill.

When you use the **Soul Steal** Skill, you may target any number of creatures at the same
time (you perform a single Check and compare it to the Magic Defense of each creature
you are attempting to steal from).
