> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Darkblade]] Class.

As long as you are suffering from one or more status effects, you gain a +1 bonus on
all Checks for every status effect you are suffering from, and you deal 2 extra damage
for every status effect you are suffering from (be it with attacks, spells, Arcana, items or any other method).
