> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[Dancer]] Class.

On your turn during a conflict scene, if you have no **martial armor** equipped, you
may use an action and spend 1 Fabula Point to launch yourself into a breathtaking
performance. If you do, you may immediately perform **up to three different dances**
among those you know, in the order you prefer and without paying their MP cost;
afterwards, **no one (not even you) can perform dances** until the end of the scene.
