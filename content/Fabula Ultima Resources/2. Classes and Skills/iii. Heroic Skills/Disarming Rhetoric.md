> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Orator]] Class.

During a conflict scene, you may use an action and choose a **soldier**-rank creature that
can hear and understand you (the Game Master must tell you who is a valid target). If
that creature is [[shaken]] or in [[Crisis]], you may spend an amount of Mind Points equal to
**20 + half that creature's level** to have them peacefully leave the conflict.

Describe how you convince them to desist.
