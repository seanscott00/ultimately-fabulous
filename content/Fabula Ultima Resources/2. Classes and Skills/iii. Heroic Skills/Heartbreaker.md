> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Darkblade]] Class.

When you hit a creature with an attack, if that attack **only** targeted that creature and
you have a **Bond** towards them, you may choose to spend half of your current Hit Points,
rounded down. If you do, the attack deals extra damage equal to **10 multiplied by the
strength of your Bond towards the target**.

You may use this Skill only on your turn during a conflict, and **only once per turn**.
