> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Arcanist]] Class.

When you successfully perform a Check to fill or erase one or more sections of a Clock,
if the **domains** of one or more Arcana you have bound are applicable to the Check in
question, you may fill or erase an additional section of that Clock.

The Game Master has final say on whether a given domain applies or not.
