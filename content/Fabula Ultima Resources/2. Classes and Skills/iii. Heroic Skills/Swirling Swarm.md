> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[Fury|Fury]] and
[[Sharpshooter|Sharpshooter]].

When you perform a **ranged** attack using a weapon that belongs to the **[[thrown|thrown]]** Category,
**if you have no martial armor and no shields equipped**, you gain **1 Momentum**.

You may never have more than **5 Momentum**.

Your **ranged** attacks using weapons that belong to the **thrown** Category deal extra
damage equal to your current Momentum.

When a creature hits you with an attack targeting your **Defense**, you **must** spend all
your accumulated Momentum and increase your Defense score by an equal amount
against that attack (this may turn the hit into a miss).

You also lose all Momentum at the end of each scene.
