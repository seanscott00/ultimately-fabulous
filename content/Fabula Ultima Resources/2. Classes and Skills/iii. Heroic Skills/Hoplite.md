> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the Guardian Class.

As long as you have a **weapon** equipped in your **main hand slot** and a **[[shield|shield]]** equipped
in your **off-hand slot**, your attacks with that weapon deal 5 extra damage and you gain
a +1 bonus to Defense.

This Skill **cannot** be combined with [[Custom Weapons|Custom Weapons]], **nor** with the [[Dual Shieldbearer|Dual Shieldbearer]] Skill.
