> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[Rogue|Rogue]],
[[Sharpshooter|Sharpshooter]], and [[Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]], and must have acquired the [[High Speed|High Speed]] Skill.

When you use **[[High Speed|High Speed]]** to perform a **free attack** with a weapon belonging to the
**[[bow|bow]]**, **[[spear|spear]]** or **[[thrown|thrown]]** Category, the attack deals extra damage equal to **[your Skill
Level in the [[High Speed|High Speed]] Skill, multiplied by 5]**.

Additionally, when you attack one or more creatures with a weapon that belongs to
the **bow**, **spear**, or **thrown** Category, those creatures cannot perform **free attacks** until
the end of the current turn.
