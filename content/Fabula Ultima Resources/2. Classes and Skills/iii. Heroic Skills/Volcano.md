> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Elementalist|Elementalist]] Class.

You learn the ultimate **Elementalist** spell: **Volcano**.
###### Volcano
| MP | TARGET | DURATION |
| --- | --- | --- |
|40|Special|Instantaneous|
You channel the fury of the planet into a powerful wave of fire and magma. Choose one
option: one creature you can see suffers 50 **fire** damage; **or** you choose any number
of creatures you can see, and each of them suffers 30 **fire** damage. These amounts
increase by 5 if you are **level 20 or higher**, or by 10 if you are **level 40 or higher**.

Damage dealt by this spell ignores Resistances and Immunities.
