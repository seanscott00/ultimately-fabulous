> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Arcanist]] Class.

You make contact with an unknown Arcanum and bind it to your soul. This Arcanum
must be something you design together with the rest of the group; as long as you live,
no one else in your world will be able to bind that Arcanum.

Once per scene while you are **merged** with an Arcanum, you may use an action
and spend 2 Fabula Points to **trigger** that Arcanum's **dismiss** effect (if any) without
dismissing them. Doing so **does not** trigger the [[Arcane Circle]] Skill.
