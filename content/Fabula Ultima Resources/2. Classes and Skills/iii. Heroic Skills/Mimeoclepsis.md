> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[Chimerist|Chimerist]] Class, and must have acquired
the [[Genoclepsis|Genoclepsis]] Skill from the [[Mutant|Mutant]] Class.

When you use the **Genoclepsis** Skill on an NPC, if that NPC's Species is among the ones
you can learn spells from with the [[Spell Mimic|Spell Mimic]] Skill,
the Game Master must reveal all **spells** known by that NPC. Then, if that NPC knows
one or more spell, you may **learn one of those spells** as a [[Chimerist|Chimerist]] spell of your own.
