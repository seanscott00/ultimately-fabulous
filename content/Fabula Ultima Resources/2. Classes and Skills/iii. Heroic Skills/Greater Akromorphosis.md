> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[Mutant|Mutant]] Class, and must have acquired the
[[Akromorphosis|Akromorphosis]] Skill.

You gain the following benefits based on the current Category of your **[[Unarmed Strike|Unarmed Strike]]s**.
- **[[arcane|arcane]], [[brawling|brawling]], [[dagger|dagger]], [[thrown|thrown]]:** your attacks with **unarmed strikes** treat the
Defense of each target as being equal to their current **Insight** die size.
- **[[bow|bow]], [[sword|sword]]:** your attacks with **unarmed strikes** that target **two or more creatures**
deal 5 extra damage.
- **[[firearm|firearm]], [[heavy|heavy]]:** your **unarmed strikes** deal 5 extra damage to **constructs**, and deal
5 extra damage to non-**construct** creatures who have at least one **shield** and/or
**martial armor** equipped.
- **[[flail|flail]], [[spear|spear]]:** you gain a +2 bonus to Defense, and your attacks with **unarmed strikes**
may target **flying** creatures.

If your **unarmed strikes** are treated as belonging to **more than one Category** (for
instance due to the [[Blade Adept|Blade Adept]] Heroic Skill),
you gain **all** the benefits of the corresponding Categories.
