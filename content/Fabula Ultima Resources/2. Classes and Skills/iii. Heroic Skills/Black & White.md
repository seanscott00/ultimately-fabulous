> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[Ace of Cards|Ace of Cards]],
[[Darkblade|Darkblade]], [[Entropist|Entropist]], and [[Spiritist|Spiritist]], and you must have acquired the [[Magic Cards|Magic Cards]] Skill.

When you resolve the [[Ace of Cards#Double Trouble|Double Trouble]] effect, you may have all of its damage become
**light** or **dark** (instead of being of a type matching the **suits** of the resolved cards).

Additionally, as long as there is a **joker** in your **discard pile**, all damage dealt by your
[[Ace of Cards#Blinding Flush|Blinding Flush]], [[Ace of Cards#Double Trouble|Double Trouble]], and [[Ace of Cards#Magic Flush|Magic Flush]] effects ignores Immunities and
Resistances.
