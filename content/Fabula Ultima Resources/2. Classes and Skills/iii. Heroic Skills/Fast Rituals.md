> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[Chimerist|Chimerist]],
[[Elementalist|Elementalist]], [[Entropist|Entropist]], and [[Spiritist|Spiritist]].

When you initiate a Ritual during a conflict scene, its Ritual Clock is created with **two
fewer sections** than normal, to a minimum of **four sections**.
