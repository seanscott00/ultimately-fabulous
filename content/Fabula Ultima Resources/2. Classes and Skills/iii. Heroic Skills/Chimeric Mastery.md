> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Chimerist]] Class.

Choose two creature Species among **construct**, **demon**, **elemental**, and **undead**. You
can now use **spell mimic** to learn spells from creatures of the chosen Species.

This Heroic Skill **may be acquired up to twice**, each time selecting two Species from
the list above.

Whenever you acquire this Skill, you also increase your upper limit for memorized
Chimerist spells by 2.
