> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[Elementalist]],
[[Entropist]], [[Invoker]], and [[Symbolist]], and must have
acquired the [[Invocation]] Skill.

After an enemy you can see **hits or misses you with an offensive spell (⚡)**, if the Result
of their Magic Check was an **even number**, you may immediately use the **Invocation**
Skill for free (after the spell has been fully resolved) to perform an **invocation** you
have access to against that enemy, without spending Mind Points; this invocation must **not** be a **"hex"**, and it may target **only** that enemy (you cannot use the
[[Linked Invocation]] Skill with it).
