> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[Ace of Cards|Ace of Cards]],
[[Fury|Fury]], [[Rogue|Rogue]], [[Sharpshooter|Sharpshooter]], and [[Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]], and you must have acquired the
[[Magic Cards|Magic Cards]] Skill.

When you resolve the [[Ace of Cards#Magic Pair|Magic Pair]] effect, if you attack with a weapon belonging to
the **[[arcane|arcane]]** or **[[thrown|thrown]]** Category, you deal extra damage equal to **the common value
of the resolved cards** and regain an amount of Mind Points equal to **twice the
common value of the resolved cards** (for instance, resolving a pair of **5s** will allow
you to deal 5 extra damage and regain 10 Mind Points).
