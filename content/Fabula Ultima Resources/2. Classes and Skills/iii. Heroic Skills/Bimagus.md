> **Requirements:** you must have mastered two or more Classes among [[Elementalist|Elementalist]],
[[Entropist|Entropist]], and [[Spiritist|Spiritist]], and your character must be level 30 or higher.

Once per turn during a conflict, if you have an **[[arcane|arcane]]** weapon equipped, you may use
an action and spend **20 to 50 Mind Points** to perform the **Spell** action for free **twice**,
casting two spells without needing to pay their MP costs. If you do, the two spells must
have a **combined total MP cost equal to or lower than 20 + the amount of MP you
spent to use this Skill**, and the second spell must have **a total MP cost that is equal
to or lower than half the total MP cost** of the first spell.
