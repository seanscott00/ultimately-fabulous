> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[Fury|Fury]] Class.

You may equip two-handed weapons belonging to the **[[flail|flail]]**, **[[heavy|heavy]]**, **[[spear|spear]]**, or **[[sword|sword]]**
Categories in a single hand slot.

This allows you, for instance, to enjoy the benefits of [[two-weapon fighting|two-weapon fighting]]
while wielding a pair of two-handed weapons, **or** to equip a two-handed weapon in
your **main hand slot** and a shield in your **off-hand slot**.
