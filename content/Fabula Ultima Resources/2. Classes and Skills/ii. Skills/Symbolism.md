*(Max Skill Level: 5)*

Each time you acquire this Skill, you learn **two symbols**.

You may have **up to** **[SL + 1]** **symbols active at the same time**; if you create more
while at your limit, you must first choose and destroy some of your previous symbols.

You may perform the **Inventory** action and spend 2 Inventory Points to create a **symbol**
you have learned and choose one option: apply that symbol to yourself; **or** apply that
symbol to an ally you can see; **or** perform a **free attack** with a weapon you have
equipped. This attack deals no damage, but you apply a copy of the chosen symbol to
each enemy hit by the attack (each copy counts as a separate symbol towards your limit
of **[SL + 1]** active symbols).
