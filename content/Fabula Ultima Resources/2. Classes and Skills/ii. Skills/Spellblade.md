*(Max Skill Level: 4)*

When you cast an offensive (<span class='fu-off'></span>) spell targeting a **single creature**, if the spell has a **total Mind Point cost of [SL × 10] or lower** and you have one or more **[[bow|bow]]**, **[[brawling|brawling]]**, **[[dagger|dagger]]**, **[[flail|flail]]**, **[[spear|spear]]** or **[[sword|sword]]** weapons equipped, you may choose one of those weapons. If you do, your Magic Check for the spell will use the chosen weapon's Accuracy Check formula.
