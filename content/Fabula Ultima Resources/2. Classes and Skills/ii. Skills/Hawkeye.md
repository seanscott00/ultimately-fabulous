*(Max Skill Level: 5)*

When you perform the **Guard** action, if you choose **not** to provide cover to another creature, you may choose one option: the next **ranged** attack you perform before the end of the current scene will deal **[SL × 2]** extra damage; **or** you may immediately perform a **free attack** with a **[[bow|bow]]** or **[[firearm|firearm]]** you have equipped, treating your **High Roll (HR) as 0 when calculating damage dealt by this attack.
