When you perform a **dance** with a **duration** of "Until the start of your next turn", you
may spend 10 additional Mind Points. If you do, choose one ally you can see towards
whom you have a Bond of **affection**: apply the benefits of the dance to that ally as well
as yourself (the benefits still last until the start of your next turn).
