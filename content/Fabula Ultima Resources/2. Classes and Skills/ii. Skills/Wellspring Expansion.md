*(Max Skill Level: 5)*

As long as you have at least one weapon belonging to the **[[arcane|arcane]]**, **[[bow|bow]]**,  **[[brawling|brawling]]**, **[[flail|flail]]**, **[[sword|sword]]**, or **[[thrown|thrown]]**
Category equipped, your **"blast" invocations** deal **[SL]** extra damage, and the extra
damage granted by your **"hex" invocations** is increased by **[SL]**.
