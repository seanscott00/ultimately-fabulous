Before you perform an Accuracy Check, a Magic Check for an offensive spell (<span class='fu-off'></span>), or a Check to advance or turn back a Clock, you may declare **double or nothing**. If you do and the Check triggers a **critical success**, **double** the damage dealt by that attack or spell or the filled or erased sections of that Clock, respectively; however, any other kind of success becomes a **failure** instead.
