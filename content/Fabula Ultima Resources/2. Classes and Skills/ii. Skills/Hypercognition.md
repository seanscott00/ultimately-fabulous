*(Max Skill Level: 5)*

The total MP cost of your **spells** and **verses** that
include your **focus** among their targets is reduced by **[SL]**, or by **[SL × 2]** if your
**focus** is the **only** target (up to a minimum cost of 0 Mind Points).
