*(Max Skill Level: 3)*

After you affect one or more enemies with a verse, if you have an [[arcane|arcane]] weapon
equipped, you may choose one option: until the start of your next turn, your allies deal **[SL]** extra damage to those enemies; or until the start of your next turn, you recover **[SL]** Mind Points every time one of those enemies suffers damage (if the same
source deals damage to two or more of those enemies at the same time, you recover **[SL]** MP for each damaged enemy).
