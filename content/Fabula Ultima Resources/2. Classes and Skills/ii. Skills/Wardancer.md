*(Max Skill Level: 5)*

After you perform a **dance**, your attacks with **[[brawling|brawling]]**, **[[dagger|dagger]]**, **[[flail|flail]]** and **[[thrown|thrown]]**
weapons deal **[SL]** extra damage until the start of your next turn; if you have an
**[[arcane|arcane]]** weapon equipped, offensive spells (<span class='fu-off'></span>) you cast also deal **[SL]** extra damage
until the start of your next turn.
