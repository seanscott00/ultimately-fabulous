You may now equip a [[shield|shield]] in your main hand slot. As long as you have two shields
equipped, you gain the benefits of both items and may treat them as the following
combined two-handed melee [[brawling|brawling]] weapon:

> [!statblock|shield] Twin Shields _[MIG + MIG] ⋄ [HR + 5] physical_
> Two-handed ⋄ Melee ⋄ Deals extra damage equal to your **[SL]** in **Defensive Mastery**.
