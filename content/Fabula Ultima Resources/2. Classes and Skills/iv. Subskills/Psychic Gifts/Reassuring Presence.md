##### Reassuring Presence
> (trigger::After you **cover** an ally with the **Guard** action)

(effect::That ally recovers an amount of Mind Points equal to **10 + (the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock, multiplied by 5)**; if they are your focus, they also recover from a single status effect of their choice among [[dazed|dazed]], [[enraged|enraged]], and [[shaken|shaken]].)
