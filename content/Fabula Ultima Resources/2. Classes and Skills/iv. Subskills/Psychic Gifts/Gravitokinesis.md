##### Gravitokinesis
> (trigger::When you **deal damage**)

(effect::That damage becomes **earth** or **physical**, and its source deals extra damage equal to **2 + the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock**. If the source deals damage to one or more **flying** creatures, they are forced to land immediately.)
