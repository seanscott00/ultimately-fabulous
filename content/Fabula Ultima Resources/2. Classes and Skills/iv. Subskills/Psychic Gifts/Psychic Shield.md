##### Psychic Shield
> (trigger::After an enemy you can see **performs an Accuracy Check or a Magic Check**)

(effect::You may treat your Defense and Magic Defense scores as being equal to **your current Willpower die size + (the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock, multiplied by 2)** against that Check (you may still use your normal scores if better).)
