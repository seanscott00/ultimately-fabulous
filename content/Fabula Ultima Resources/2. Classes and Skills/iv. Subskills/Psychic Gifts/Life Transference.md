##### Life Transference
> (trigger::When you cause **one or more creatures to lose HP**)

(effect::Choose yourself **or** an ally who is your **focus**: if the chosen creature is in [[Crisis]], they recover an amount of Hit Points equal to **5 + (the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock, multiplied by 5)**.)
