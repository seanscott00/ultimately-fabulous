##### Clairvoyance
> (trigger::When an NPC becomes your **focus** or when you create a **Bond** towards an NPC)

(effect::You ask the Game Master a single question about that NPC, and the Game Master answers truthfully; then, you describe what sensation revealed this information to you. You may only use this gift **once** on the same NPC.)
