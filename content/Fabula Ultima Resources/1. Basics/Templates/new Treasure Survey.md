## Instructions
- Each section in the tables is for one Player Character.
- Each Player should write down some ideas for equipment they’d like their character to get throughout the game. Pick from the sample rare items in the book and rename them to fit your character, or design new ones collaboratively with the rest of the group.
- You don’t have to fill out every slot—in fact, it’s probably best to leave some space for ideas you’ll have later!

### Reference
- Designing Rare Items/Sample Rare Items: **Core Rulebook**, page **266**.
- Rare Items: **High Fantasy Atlas**, page **76**.
- Custom Weapons: **High Fantasy Atlas**, page **106**.
- Materials, Forging, and additional Qualities: **Winter Holiday Special 2022**.
