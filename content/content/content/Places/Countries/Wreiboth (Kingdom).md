## History

#### The Calamity and the formation of the Crystal Wastes

Approximately one year ago, in 378 , Wreiboth suffered tragedy as an unknown calamity occurred in and around the island, with earthquakes and chaotic magic storms rending the land, leaving large portions of the country under water, and other large portions being overgrown with large, jagged, crystalline formations, leaving the land mostly unusable. The capital city of [[|]] is gone, caught at the center of the incident, leaving the government and society in tatters. With the destruction of the palace and the loss of the majority of the royal family, the King's third son, [[|]], who had previously been 5th in the line of succession after his three older sisters and older brother, assumed the crown and the throne in exile.
