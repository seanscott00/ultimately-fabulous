> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[../i. Classes/Entropist|Entropist]] and
[[../i. Classes/Necromancer|Necromancer]], and must have learned both the [[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Drain Spirit|Drain Spirit]] and [[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Drain Vigor|Drain Vigor]] spells.

When you cast the **Drain Spirit** or **Drain Vigor** spells, the amount of Mind Points or Hit
Points you recover is **equal** to the MP or HP loss suffered by the target, respectively
(instead of being halved).

Additionally, your **Drain Spirit** causes the target to lose **[HR + 20]** Mind Points if you
are **level 20 or higher**, or **[HR + 25]** Mind Points if you are **level 40 or higher**, instead
of the normal **[HR + 15]**.
