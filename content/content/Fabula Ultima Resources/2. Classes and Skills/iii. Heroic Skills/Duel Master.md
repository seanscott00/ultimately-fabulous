> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[../i. Classes/Ace of Cards|Ace of Cards]],
[[../i. Classes/Fury|Fury]], [[../i. Classes/Rogue|Rogue]], [[../i. Classes/Sharpshooter|Sharpshooter]], and [[../i. Classes/Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]], and you must have acquired the
[[../ii. Skills/Magic Cards|Magic Cards]] Skill.

When you resolve the [[../i. Classes/Ace of Cards#Magic Pair|Magic Pair]] effect, if you attack with a weapon belonging to
the **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/arcane|arcane]]** or **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/thrown|thrown]]** Category, you deal extra damage equal to **the common value
of the resolved cards** and regain an amount of Mind Points equal to **twice the
common value of the resolved cards** (for instance, resolving a pair of **5s** will allow
you to deal 5 extra damage and regain 10 Mind Points).
