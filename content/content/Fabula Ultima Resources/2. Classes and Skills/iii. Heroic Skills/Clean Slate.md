> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Symbolist|Symbolist]] Class.

You may use an action and spend up to 30 Mind Points to choose one creature you
can see for every 5 MP spent this way. Each creature chosen this way recovers from all
status effects and is no longer affected by any spells with a **duration** of "Scene" that
were affecting them. If a creature chosen this way bears one or more symbols, those
symbols are destroyed.
