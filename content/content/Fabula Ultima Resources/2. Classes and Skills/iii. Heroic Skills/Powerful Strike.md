> **Requirements:** You must have mastered one or more Classes among the following:
[[../i. Classes/Fury|Fury]] or [[../i. Classes/Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]].

When you hit one or more creatures with a **melee** attack, that attack deals 5 extra
damage to each creature.

The amount of extra damage increases to 10 if you are **level 40 or higher**.
