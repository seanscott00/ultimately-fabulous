> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[../i. Classes/Chimerist|Chimerist]],
[[../i. Classes/Elementalist|Elementalist]], [[../i. Classes/Entropist|Entropist]], and [[../i. Classes/Spiritist|Spiritist]].

When you initiate a Ritual during a conflict scene, its Ritual Clock is created with **two
fewer sections** than normal, to a minimum of **four sections**.
