When you acquire this Skill, learn any two spells from one of the following lists:
[[../i. Classes/Elementalist#ELEMENTALIST SPELLS|Elementalist]], [[../i. Classes/Entropist#ENTROPIST SPELLS|Entropist]], or [[../i. Classes/Spiritist#SPIRITIST SPELLS|Spiritist]]. Both spells chosen this way must come from the
same list, and they follow the standard rules for casting spells of that Class.
