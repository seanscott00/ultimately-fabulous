> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Sharpshooter|Sharpshooter]] Class.

After you negate a **ranged** attack with the [[../ii. Skills/Crossfire|Crossfire]] Skill, if the Result of the Accuracy
Check was an **even number**, you may perform a **free attack** against the attacker with
a **ranged [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/firearm|firearm]]** weapon you have equipped. This attack must have that enemy as its
**only** target; treat your **High Roll (HR)** as 0 when calculating damage dealt by it.
