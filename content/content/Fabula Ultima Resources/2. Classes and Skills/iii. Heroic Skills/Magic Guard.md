> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[../i. Classes/Chimerist|Chimerist]],
[[../i. Classes/Elementalist|Elementalist]], [[../i. Classes/Entropist|Entropist]], and [[../i. Classes/Spiritist|Spiritist]].

When you perform the **Guard** action, if you choose **not** to provide cover to another
creature, you gain the following benefit until the start of your next turn: when a
creature you can see casts a spell, after that creature pays for the spell's MP cost, you
may spend an amount of Mind Points equal to **10 + the spell's total MP cost** in
order to completely stop the casting of the spell and negate its effects (the spell isn't
cast, but the creature is still considered to have spent their action).
