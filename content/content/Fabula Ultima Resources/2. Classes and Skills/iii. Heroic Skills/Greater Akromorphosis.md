> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Mutant|Mutant]] Class, and must have acquired the
[[../ii. Skills/Akromorphosis|Akromorphosis]] Skill.

You gain the following benefits based on the current Category of your **[[../../3. Items and Equipment/Ii. Basic Equipment/Unarmed Strike|Unarmed Strike]]s**.
- **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/arcane|arcane]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/brawling|brawling]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/dagger|dagger]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/thrown|thrown]]:** your attacks with **unarmed strikes** treat the
Defense of each target as being equal to their current **Insight** die size.
- **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/bow|bow]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/sword|sword]]:** your attacks with **unarmed strikes** that target **two or more creatures**
deal 5 extra damage.
- **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/firearm|firearm]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/heavy|heavy]]:** your **unarmed strikes** deal 5 extra damage to **constructs**, and deal
5 extra damage to non-**construct** creatures who have at least one **shield** and/or
**martial armor** equipped.
- **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/flail|flail]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/spear|spear]]:** you gain a +2 bonus to Defense, and your attacks with **unarmed strikes**
may target **flying** creatures.

If your **unarmed strikes** are treated as belonging to **more than one Category** (for
instance due to the [[./Blade Adept|Blade Adept]] Heroic Skill),
you gain **all** the benefits of the corresponding Categories.
