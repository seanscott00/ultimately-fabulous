> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[../i. Classes/Chimerist|Chimerist]],
[[../i. Classes/Darkblade|Darkblade]], [[../i. Classes/Esper|Esper]], [[../i. Classes/Fury|Fury]], [[../i. Classes/Guardian|Guardian]], and [[../i. Classes/Wayfarer|Wayfarer]].

When you perform a Magic Check for a spell or Ritual, you may replace **one** of the
Attributes with **Might** (such as [MIG + MIG] for a Chimerism Ritual).

As long as the **Accuracy** formula of a weapon you have equipped includes **Might**, you
may treat that weapon as also belonging to the **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/arcane|arcane]]** Category for the purpose of
Skills and effects that require it.
