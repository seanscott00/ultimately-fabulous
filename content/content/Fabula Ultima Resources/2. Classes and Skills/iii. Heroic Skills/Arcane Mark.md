> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[../i. Classes/Sharpshooter|Sharpshooter]]
and [[../i. Classes/Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]].

After you hit one or more creatures with a **melee** or **ranged** attack using a weapon
that belongs to the **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/arcane|arcane]]** Category, you may choose one of those creatures and apply
an **arcane mark** on them. When you cast an offensive (<span class='fu-off'></span>) spell that has the marked
creature among its targets, that spell's **total MP cost** is reduced by 10, to a minimum
of 5. This effect lasts until the end of your next turn, or until you use this Skill again.
