> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Orator|Orator]] Class.

Once per turn during a conflict, after you use the [[../ii. Skills/Condemn|Condemn]] Skill or the [[../ii. Skills/Encourage|Encourage]] Skill,
you may immediately perform that same Skill again (on the same target or a different
one). You must still pay the Mind Point cost for the second use of the Skill.
