> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more classes amon [[../i. Classes/Fury|Fury]] and  [[../i. Classes/Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]].

When you perform the **Attack** action with a **melee** weapon that belongs to the **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/flail|flail]]**
Category, if you have **no other weapon equipped**, you may perform two separate
attacks instead of one (against the same target or against different targets). If you do,
both attacks follow the rules for [[two-weapon fighting|two-weapon fighting]]: each attack loses the **multi**
property and cannot gain it, and you treat the High Roll of each Accuracy Check as
being equal to 0 when determining damage.

Note that this Heroic Skill **does not stack** with the Quick customization from custom
weapons (using **Spider's Web** with a Quick flail doesn't allow you to make three or
more attacks, basically).
