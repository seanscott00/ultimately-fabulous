> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]] Class.

When you perform a **melee** attack with the **multi** property, if you choose to target only
one creature, the attack deals 5 extra damage if the attack had **multi (2)**, or 10 extra
damage if the attack had **multi (3 or higher)**.
