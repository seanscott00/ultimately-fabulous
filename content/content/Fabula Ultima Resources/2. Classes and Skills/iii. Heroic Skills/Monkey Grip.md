> **Requirements:** You must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Fury|Fury]] Class.

You may equip two-handed weapons belonging to the **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/flail|flail]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/heavy|heavy]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/spear|spear]]**, or **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/sword|sword]]**
Categories in a single hand slot.

This allows you, for instance, to enjoy the benefits of [[two-weapon fighting|two-weapon fighting]]
while wielding a pair of two-handed weapons, **or** to equip a two-handed weapon in
your **main hand slot** and a shield in your **off-hand slot**.
