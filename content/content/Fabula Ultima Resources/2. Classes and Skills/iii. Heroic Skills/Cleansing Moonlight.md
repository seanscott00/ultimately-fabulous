> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[../i. Classes/Entropist|Entropist]] and
[[../i. Classes/Spiritist|Spiritist]].

When you hit one or more creatures with an offensive spell (<span class='fu-off'></span>), if you have an **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/arcane|arcane]]**
weapon equipped and the **High Roll** of your **Magic Check** is **8 or higher**, you may have
each of those creatures no longer be affected by **all** spells with a duration of "Scene"
that are currently affecting them.

When you cast a spell targeting one or more allies, if you have an **arcane** weapon
equipped, you may choose one status effect among [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/dazed|dazed]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/enraged|enraged]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/poisoned|poisoned]], [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/shaken|shaken]],
[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/slow|slow]], and [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/weak|weak]]: if you do, each of the spell's targets recovers from the chosen status
effect.
