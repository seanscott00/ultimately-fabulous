> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Gourmet|Gourmet]] Class.

Once per scene when you use the **[[../ii. Skills/Cooking|Cooking]]** Skill, you may combine **four ingredients
into a delicacy** (thus ignoring the normal limit of three ingredients). Other than that,
the delicacy follows the normal rules.
