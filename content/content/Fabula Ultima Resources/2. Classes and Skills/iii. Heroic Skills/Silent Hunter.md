> **Requirements:** you must have mastered one or more Classes among [[../i. Classes/Rogue|Rogue]],
[[../i. Classes/Sharpshooter|Sharpshooter]], and [[../i. Classes/Weaponmaster|Weaponmaster]], and must have acquired the [[../ii. Skills/High Speed|High Speed]] Skill.

When you use **[[../ii. Skills/High Speed|High Speed]]** to perform a **free attack** with a weapon belonging to the
**[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/bow|bow]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/spear|spear]]** or **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/thrown|thrown]]** Category, the attack deals extra damage equal to **[your Skill
Level in the [[../ii. Skills/High Speed|High Speed]] Skill, multiplied by 5]**.

Additionally, when you attack one or more creatures with a weapon that belongs to
the **bow**, **spear**, or **thrown** Category, those creatures cannot perform **free attacks** until
the end of the current turn.
