> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Necromancer|Necromancer]] Class.

As long as you have **at least 1 Grave Point**, you gain Immunity to **dark** and **poison**
damage, Resistance to **physical** damage, and Vulnerability to **light** damage. These
Affinities **overwrite** any other Affinity for those damage types, and cannot be altered **in
any way** as long as you have at least 1 Grave Point.
