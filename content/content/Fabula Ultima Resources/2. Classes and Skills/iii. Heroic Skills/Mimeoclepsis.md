> **Requirements:** you must have mastered the [[../i. Classes/Chimerist|Chimerist]] Class, and must have acquired
the [[../ii. Skills/Genoclepsis|Genoclepsis]] Skill from the [[../i. Classes/Mutant|Mutant]] Class.

When you use the **Genoclepsis** Skill on an NPC, if that NPC's Species is among the ones
you can learn spells from with the [[../ii. Skills/Spell Mimic|Spell Mimic]] Skill,
the Game Master must reveal all **spells** known by that NPC. Then, if that NPC knows
one or more spell, you may **learn one of those spells** as a [[../i. Classes/Chimerist|Chimerist]] spell of your own.
