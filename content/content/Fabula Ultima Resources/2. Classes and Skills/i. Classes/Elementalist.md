# Elementalist Spells

> [!list|spells no-title]
>  - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Elemental Shroud|Elemental Shroud]]** ~ **5 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Scene**<br>Choose a damage type: **air**, **bolt**, **earth**, **fire** or **ice**. Until this spell ends, each target gains Resistance against the chosen damage type.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Elemental Weapon|Elemental Weapon]]** ~ **10 MP** ~ **One weapon** ~ **Scene**<br>Choose a damage type: **air**, **bolt**, **earth**, **fire**, or **ice**. Until this spell ends, all damage dealt by the weapon becomes of the chosen damage type. If you have that weapon equipped while you cast this spell, you may perform a **free attack** with it as part of the same action. This spell can only be cast on a weapon equipped by a willing creature.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Flare|Flare]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **20 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>The target suffers **[HR + 25]** **fire** damage. Damage dealt by this spell ignores Resistances.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Fulgur|Fulgur]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target hit by this spell suffers **[HR + 15]** **bolt** damage. **Opportunity:** Each target hit by this spell suffers [[dazed|dazed]].
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Glacies|Glacies]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target hit by this spell suffers **[HR + 15]** **ice** damage. **Opportunity:** Each target hit by this spell suffers [[slow|slow]].
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Iceberg|Iceberg]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **20 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>The target suffers **[HR + 25]** **ice** damage. Damage dealt by this spell ignores Resistances.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Ignis|Ignis]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target hit by this spell suffers **[HR + 15]** **fire** damage. **Opportunity:** Each target hit by this spell suffers [[shaken|shaken]].
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Soaring Strike|Soaring Strike]]** ~ **10 MP** ~ **Self** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>You may immediately perform a **free attack** with a **melee** weapon you have equipped. This attack may target creatures that can only be targeted by **ranged** attacks. If you used a weapon belonging to the **[[brawling|brawling]]** or **[[spear|spear]]** Category for this attack, it deals 5 extra damage. If you hit a flying target with this attack, you may force them to land immediately.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Terra|Terra]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target hit by this spell suffers **[HR + 15]** **earth** damage. This spell cannot target creatures who are flying, floating, falling, or otherwise in mid-air. **Opportunity:** Each target hit by this spell performs one fewer action on their next turn, to a minimum of 0 actions.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Thunderbolt|Thunderbolt]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **20 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>The target suffers **[HR + 25]** **bolt** damage. Damage dealt by this spell ignores Resistances.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Ventus|Ventus]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target hit by this spell suffers **[HR + 15]** **air** damage. **Opportunity:** Each flying target hit by this spell is forced to land immediately.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Elementalist Spells/Vortex|Vortex]]** ~ **10 MP** ~ **Self** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, you gain a +2 bonus to your Defense against **ranged** attacks.
