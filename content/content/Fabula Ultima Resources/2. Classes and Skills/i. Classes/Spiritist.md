# Spiritist Spells

> [!list|spells no-title]
>  - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Aura|Aura]]** ~ **5 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, each target may treat their Magic Defense as being equal to 12 against any effects that target it (they are still free to use their normal Defense score if higher than 12).
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Awaken|Awaken]]** ~ **20 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Scene**<br>Choose one Attribute: **Dexterity**, **Insight**, **Might**, or **Willpower**. Until this spell ends, the target treats the chosen Attribute as if it were one die size higher (up to a maximum of **d12**).
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Barrier|Barrier]]** ~ **5 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, each target may treat their Defense as being equal to 12 against any effects that target it (they are still free to use their normal Defense score if higher than 12).
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Cleanse|Cleanse]]** ~ **5 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target recovers from all status effects.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Enrage|Enrage]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>The target suffers [[enraged|enraged]] and cannot perform the **Guard** or **Spell** actions during their next turn.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Hallucination|Hallucination]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **5 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Choose [[dazed|dazed]] or [[shaken|shaken]]: you inflict the chosen status effect on each target hit by this spell.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Heal|Heal]]** ~ **10 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target recovers 40 Hit Points. This amount increases to 50 Hit Points if you are **level 20 or higher**, or to 60 Hit Points if you are **level 40 or higher**.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Lux|Lux]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target hit by this spell suffers **[HR + 15] light** damage. **Opportunity:** Each target hit by this spell suffers [[dazed|dazed]].
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Mercy|Mercy]]** ~ ** MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, if the target would be reduced to 0 Hit Points, they are instead left standing with exactly 1 Hit Point. Once that happens, this spell ends.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Reinforce|Reinforce]]** ~ **5 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Scene**<br>Choose [[dazed|dazed]], [[enraged|enraged]], [[poisoned|poisoned]], [[shaken|shaken]], [[slow|slow]], or [[weak|weak]]. Until this spell ends, each target becomes immune to the chosen status effect.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Soul Weapon|Soul Weapon]]** ~ **10 MP** ~ **One equipped weapon** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, all damage dealt by the weapon becomes of the **light** type. If you have that weapon equipped while you cast this spell, you may perform a **free attack** with it as part of the same action. This spell can only be cast on a weapon equipped by a willing creature.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Spiritist Spells/Torpor|Torpor]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **5 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Choose [[slow|slow]] or [[weak|weak]]: you inflict the chosen status effect on each target hit by this spell.
