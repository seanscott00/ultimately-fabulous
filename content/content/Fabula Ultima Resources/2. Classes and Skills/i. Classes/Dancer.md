## Dances

- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Angel Dance|Angel Dance]]** ⋄ *Until the start of your next turn*<br>Choose one option: you gain Resistance to **light** damage; **or** all damage dealt by your attacks and spells becomes **light**.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Banshee Dance|Banshee Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>Choose another creature that is able to see you; if that creature is already [[slow|slow]], they immediately suffer [[shaken|shaken]].
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Bat Dance|Bat Dance]]** ⋄ *Until the start of your next turn*<br>Choose one option: you gain Resistance to **dark** damage; **or** all damage dealt by your attacks and spells becomes **dark**.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Golem Dance|Golem Dance]]** ⋄ *Until the start of your next turn*<br>Choose one option: you gain Resistance to **bolt** damage; **or** all damage dealt by your attacks and spells becomes **bolt**.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Griffin Dance|Griffin Dance]]** ⋄ *Until the start of your next turn*<br>Choose one option: you gain Resistance to **air** damage; **or** all damage dealt by your attacks and spells becomes **air**.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Hydra Dance|Hydra Dance]]** ⋄ *Until the start of your next turn*<br>After you suffer damage, choose one option: you recover 5 Hit Points, **or** you recover 5 Mind Points.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Kraken Dance|Kraken Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>Choose another creature that is able to see you; if that creature is already [[dazed|dazed]], they immediately suffer [[slow|slow]].
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Lion Dance|Lion Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>You immediately recover from a single status effect of your choice.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Maenad Dance|Maenad Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>Choose another creature that is able to see you; that creature loses an amount of Mind Points equal to your current **Dexterity** die size.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Myrmidon Dance|Myrmidon Dance]]** ⋄ *Until the start of your next turn*<br>Choose one option: you gain Resistance to **earth** damage; **or** all damage dealt by your attacks and spells becomes **earth**.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Nightmare Dance|Nightmare Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>Choose another creature that is able to see you; if that creature is already [[shaken|shaken]], they immediately suffer [[weak|weak]].
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Ouroboros Dance|Ouroboros Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>Choose one ally you can see who has yet to take a turn during this round. That ally may take their turn immediately after yours during this round.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Peacock Dance|Peacock Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>Choose another creature that is able to see you; the next time that creature performs an attack or casts an offensive (⚡) spell during this scene, that attack or spell must include you among its targets if possible.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Phoenix Dance|Phoenix Dance]]** ⋄ *Until the start of your next turn*<br>Choose one option: you gain Resistance to **fire** damage; **or** all damage dealt by your attacks and spells becomes **fire**.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Satyr Dance|Satyr Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>Choose another creature that is able to see you; if that creature is already [[weak|weak]], they immediately suffer [[dazed|dazed]].
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Unicorn Dance|Unicorn Dance]]** ⋄ *Instantaneous*<br>Choose an ally that is able to see you and has a Bond towards you: you and that ally both recover an amount of Hit Points equal to your current **Dexterity** die size. The restored amount increases by 5 Hit Points if you are **level 20 or higher**, or by 10 Hit Points if you are **level 40 or higher**.
- **[[../iv. Subskills/Dances/Yeti Dance|Yeti Dance]]** ⋄ *Until the start of your next turn*<br>Choose one option: you gain Resistance to **ice** damage; **or** all damage dealt by your attacks and spells becomes **ice**.
