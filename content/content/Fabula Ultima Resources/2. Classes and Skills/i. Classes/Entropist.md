# Entropist Spells

> [!list|spells no-title]
>  - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Acceleration|Acceleration]]** ~ **20 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, the target gains the ability to perform a single additional action during each of their turns. Once the target has performed a total of two additional actions granted by this spell, this spell ends.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Anomaly|Anomaly]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **20 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, if the target would suffer damage of a type they Absorb or are Immune to, they are instead treated as if they were Vulnerable to that damage type. Once that happens, this spell ends.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Dark Weapon|Dark Weapon]]** ~ **10 MP** ~ **One equipped weapon** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, all damage dealt by the weapon becomes of the **dark** type. If you have that weapon equipped while you cast this spell, you may perform a **free attack** with it as part of the same action. This spell can only be cast on a weapon equipped by a willing creature.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Dispel|Dispel]]** ~ **10 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>If the target is affected by one or more spells with a **duration** of Scene, they are no longer affected by any of those spells instead.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Divination|Divination]]** ~ **10 MP** ~ **Self** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, after a creature you can see performs a Check, if it was not a **fumble** nor a **critical success**, you may force that creature to reroll **both** dice. Once you have forced **two** rerolls this way, this spell ends.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Drain Spirit|Drain Spirit]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **5 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>The target loses **[HR + 15]** Mind Points. Then, you recover an amount of Mind Points equal to half the Mind Points loss they suffered (if the loss was reduced to 0 in some way, you recover none).
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Drain Vigor|Drain Vigor]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>The target suffers **[HR + 15] dark** damage. Then, you recover an amount of Hit Points equal to half the Hit Points loss they suffered (if the loss was reduced to 0 in some way, you recover none).
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Gamble|Gamble]]** ~ **up to 20 MP** ~ **Special** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Roll your current **Willpower** die once for every 10 Mind Points spent while casting this spell, then keep the **single** die you prefer: the number on that die determines the effects of this spell.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Mirror|Mirror]]** ~ **10 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Scene**<br>Until this spell ends, if an offensive (<span class="fu-off"></span>) spell is cast on the target, the creature who cast that offensive spell will be targeted in their stead (any other targets of the offensive spell will be targeted as normal). Once that happens, this spell ends.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Omega|Omega]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **20 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>The target loses an amount of Hit Points equal to **[20 + half the target's level]**.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Stop|Stop]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 MP** ~ **One creature** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>The target will perform one fewer action on their next turn, to a minimum of 0 actions.
> - **[[../iv. Subskills/Entropist Spells/Umbra|Umbra]]<span class='fu-off'></span>** ~ **10 × T MP** ~ **Up to three creatures** ~ **Instantaneous**<br>Each target hit by this spell suffers **[HR + 15]** **dark** damage. **Opportunity:** Each target hit by this spell suffers [[weak|weak]].
