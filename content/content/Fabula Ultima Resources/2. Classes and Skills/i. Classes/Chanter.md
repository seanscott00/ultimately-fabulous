## Tones
- ==**[[../iv. Subskills/Magichant/Calm|Calm]]**== ⋄ Each target **[recovers key]** recovery equal to **10 + twice your current Willpower die size.** This amount increases by 10 if you are **level 20 or higher**, or by 20 if you are **level 40 or higher**. If this tone causes MP recovery, it has **no effect** on the character who sings it.
- ==**[[../iv. Subskills/Magichant/Energetic|Energetic]]**== ⋄ Until the start of your next turn, when a target succeeds on a Check that includes **[key Attribute]** and that Check allows them to advance or turn back a Clock, they may fill or erase an additional section of that Clock.
- ==**[[../iv. Subskills/Magichant/Frantic|Frantic]]**== ⋄ Each target suffers **[key type]** damage equal to **twice** your current **Willpower** die size. This tone deals 10 extra damage if you are **level 20 or higher**, or 20 extra damage if you are **level 40 or higher**.
- ==**[[../iv. Subskills/Magichant/Haunting|Haunting]]**== ⋄ Each target suffers **[key status effect]**. Each target also loses Resistance to **[key type]** damage (if they have it) until the start of your next turn.
- ==**[[../iv. Subskills/Magichant/Lively|Lively]]**== ⋄ Each target treats their **[key Attribute]** as being one die size higher (up to a maximum of **d12**) until the start of your next turn.
- ==**[[../iv. Subskills/Magichant/Menacing|Menacing]]**== ⋄ The first time each target suffers damage before the start of your next turn, that damage becomes **[key type]** (this can trigger once for each target of the verse, separately).
- ==**[[../iv. Subskills/Magichant/Solemn|Solemn]]**== ⋄ Each target recovers from **[key status effect]**. Each target also gains Resistance to **[key type]** damage until the start of your next turn.
