##### Psychic Backlash
> (trigger::After an enemy **succeeds on an Opposed Check** against you or **causes you to lose Hit Points**)

(effect::That enemy loses an amount of Mind Points equal to **5 + (the number of filled sections in your Brainwave Clock, multiplied by 5)**. Then, choose one option: that enemy suffers [[../../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/dazed|dazed]]; **or** that enemy suffers [[../../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/shaken|shaken]].)
