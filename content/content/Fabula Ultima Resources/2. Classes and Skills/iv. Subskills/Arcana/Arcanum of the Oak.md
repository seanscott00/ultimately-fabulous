##### ARCANUM OF THE OAK
**Domains:** earth, plants, poison.

***MERGE***
You have Resistance to **earth** and **poison** damage and are immune to [[../../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/poisoned|poisoned]].
Whenever you recover Hit Points, you recover 5 extra Hit Points.

***DISMISS*** 
**Blossom.** Choose any number of creatures you can see (you may also choose yourself): each of them recovers from the [[../../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/poisoned|poisoned]] status effect and recovers 40 Hit Points. This amount increases to 50 Hit Points if you are **level 20 or higher**, or to 60 Hit Points if you are **level 40 or higher**.
