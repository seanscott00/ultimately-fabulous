##### ARCANUM OF THE WHEEL
**Domains:** destiny, speed, time.

***MERGE***
You are immune to [[../../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/slow|slow]].
You gain a +1 bonus to your Defense.

***DISMISS***
**Time Freeze.** Choose any number of creatures you can see: each of them suffers [[../../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/slow|slow]]. If a creature chosen this way is already [[../../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/slow|slow]], that creature will instead perform one fewer action during their next turn (to a minimum of 0 actions).
