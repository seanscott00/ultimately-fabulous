After you use **[[Bishop's Edict|Bishop's Edict]]**, **[[./Charging Cavalry|Charging Cavalry]]** or **[[King's Castle|King's Castle]]**, you may choose
another Player Character that can hear you who has yet to take a turn during this
round. That character may take their turn immediately after yours during this round.
