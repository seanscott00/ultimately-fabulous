*(Max Skill Level: 5)*

After you deal damage to one or more creatures with a spell, if you have an **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/arcane|arcane]]**,
**[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/dagger|dagger]]** or **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/flail|flail]]** weapon equipped, you recover **[SL × 2]** Mind Points.
