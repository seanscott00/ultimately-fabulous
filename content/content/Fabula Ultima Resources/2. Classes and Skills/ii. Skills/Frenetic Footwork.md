*(Max Skill Level: 2)*

After you perform a **dance** with a **duration** of "Until the start of your next turn", you gain
a bonus equal to **[SL × 2]** to all Opposed Checks that rely on **acrobatics**, **coordination**
or **speed** until the start of your next turn.
