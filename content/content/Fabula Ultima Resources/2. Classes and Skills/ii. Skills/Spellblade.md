*(Max Skill Level: 4)*

When you cast an offensive (<span class='fu-off'></span>) spell targeting a **single creature**, if the spell has a **total Mind Point cost of [SL × 10] or lower** and you have one or more **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/bow|bow]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/brawling|brawling]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/dagger|dagger]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/flail|flail]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/spear|spear]]** or **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/sword|sword]]** weapons equipped, you may choose one of those weapons. If you do, your Magic Check for the spell will use the chosen weapon's Accuracy Check formula.
