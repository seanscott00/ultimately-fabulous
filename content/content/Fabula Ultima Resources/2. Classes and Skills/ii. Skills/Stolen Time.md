*(Max Skill Level: 4)*

During a conflict, you may use an action to interfere with the flow of time by spending up to **[SL × 5]** Mind Points. For every 5 Mind Points you spend this way, choose one option: one creature you can see suffers [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/slow|slow]]; **or** one creature you can see recovers from [[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Status Effects/slow|slow]]; **or** one creature you can see may immediately perform the **Equipment** action for free; **or** choose one ally you can see who has yet to take a turn during this round: that ally may take their turn immediately after yours during this round.

Each option can only be chosen once per use of this Skill.
