*(Max Skill Level: 5)*

After you perform a **dance**, your attacks with **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/brawling|brawling]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/dagger|dagger]]**, **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/flail|flail]]** and **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/thrown|thrown]]**
weapons deal **[SL]** extra damage until the start of your next turn; if you have an
**[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/arcane|arcane]]** weapon equipped, offensive spells (<span class='fu-off'></span>) you cast also deal **[SL]** extra damage
until the start of your next turn.
