*(Max Skill Level: 4)*

During a conflict, you may use an action and spend 10 Mind Points to choose one
option: no creature can recover Hit Points or Mind Points; **or** effects that restore Hit
Points and/or Mind Points restore **[SL × 5]** additional HP and/or MP, respectively. The
chosen effect lasts until the start of your next turn.
