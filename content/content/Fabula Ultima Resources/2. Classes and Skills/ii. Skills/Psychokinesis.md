When you perform an Accuracy Check, you may replace **one** of the Attribute dice with
**Willpower** (such as **[DEX + WLP]** for a **shortbow**). Additionally, your attacks with
weapons belonging to the **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/arcane|arcane]]** or **[[../../1. Basics/Miscellaneous/Weapon Types/sword|sword]]** category may target **flying** creatures.
